# Lightsail Business Context

## Executive Summary

Lightsail is a compliance automation platform targeting startups and SMBs in the US, UK, and Nigeria. The platform helps businesses achieve SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and NDPR compliance through automated evidence collection, policy management, and continuous monitoring.

### Key Differentiators
1. **40-60% cheaper** than enterprise competitors (Vanta, Drata)
2. **First-class NDPR support** for Nigerian market
3. **AI-native** policy generation and gap analysis
4. **Developer-friendly** integrations
